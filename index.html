    My name is Teddy Bien-Aime, a college freshman freelance photographer at Morehouse College. I picked up a camera for the first time when I was in 6th grade in Port-au-Prince, Haiti, and was fascinated by the number of things that were possible with the device. Unfortunately, I had to stop due to not having my own device but didn't give up and started taking pictures with my phone. I started using my own camera in my first year in college and wanted to create a name for myself throughout my journey of photography. One morning while I was thinking of names for my business, I realized that using my initials "TB" in it somewhere could connect me to my passion. Once I shared the idea with my dorm mates, and they agreed, TB Visuals came to reality.
